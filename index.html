About Me

My Background

I'm a Front End Developer working towards my back end certifcations! I've always had an interest in creating websites and making mobile applications. Ever since high school I always thought to myself "what if there was an app to make things easier?", so I decided to get into code so I can start making my own!

My background is primarily in retail management and I'm always looking for ways to streamline efficiency, so creating websites and mobile apps just seemed like the natural next step.

I'm a huge fan of traveling and learning about different cultures, so I'm excited that web and mobile development will allow me the abilty to travel and meet more developers from across the globe.

My goal with coding is to help make safer spaces online and offline for People of Color and my LGBTQ+ family. I can't wait for you to continue on this journey with me!
